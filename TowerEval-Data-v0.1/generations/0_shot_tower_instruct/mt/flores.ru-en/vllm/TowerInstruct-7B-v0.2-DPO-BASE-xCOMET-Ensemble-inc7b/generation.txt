This new environment has different resources and different competitors, so the new population will need different characteristics or changes compared to what it had before in order to become a strong competitor.
The original population has not changed at all – it still needs the same adaptations as before.
Over time, as a new population begins to adapt to its new environment, it starts to look less and less like the rest of the population.
